Advice on opening a new steak restaurant:

In the aspect of steaks:

1.Focus on tomahawk, skirt, hanger and flank steaks at first and emphasize them on your menu

2.Filet, ribeye, strip and porterhouse steaks are not bad to consider

3.Make sirloin, round and cube steaks inconspicuous on your menu or avoid offering them

For food other than steaks:

1.Set a more varied range of wines and beers offered at your restaurant

2.Make sure the egg and cheese served at your restaurant taste great

3.Consider hiring patissiers and serve desserts

4.Salad is important

In the aspect of non-food items:

1.Set up a proper reservation system is important

2.Offer well-designed attire to your waiters/waitresses

3.Look for possible locations with outdoor seating

4.Don’t provide food delivery service

5.Avoid investing in WiFi or soundproof materials at the beginning

6.Allocating too much spaces for groups specially is unnecessary



Advice on improving an existed steak restaurant:

In the aspect of steaks:

1.Try to improve tomahawk, skirt, hanger and flank steaks if one of them has brought your restaurant many low-star comments since the customers are not so picky about them

2.Advertise your sirloin, round and cube steaks if one of them has brought your restaurant high-star comments. It is very praiseworthy to have highly rated sirloin, round and cube steaks

For food other than steaks:

1.Improve the quality of wines and beers

2.Cheese and salad are always focus points

3.Start serving desserts may help

Salad is important

In the aspect of non-food items:

1.Improve or set up your reservation system

2.Pay attention to the attire of your waiters/waitresses

3.Check if it’s possible to offer outdoor seating

4.Don’t provide food delivery service

5.Allocating too much spaces for groups specially is unnecessary
